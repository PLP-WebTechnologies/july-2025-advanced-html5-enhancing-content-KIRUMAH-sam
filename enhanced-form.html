Featured Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • Fresh black pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs with grated cheese and pepper.
  4. Drain pasta and mix quickly with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately.

Nutrition Facts

Nutrient Amount per Serving
Calories 420 kcal
Protein 18 g
Fat 17 g
Carbohydrates 48 g
